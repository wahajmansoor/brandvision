# **App Name**: Brand Vision In Seconds

## Core Features:

- Business Info Input: Collect business name, description, industry, and location via a user-friendly form.
- Logo Upload: Allow users to upload their existing logo (PNG, JPG, SVG).
- AI-Powered Brand Kit Generation: Use the business info as a tool for generating a basic brand kit including color palettes, typography suggestions, and mood board ideas.
- Brand Kit Display: Show the generated brand kit in an easy-to-understand visual format.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Blue (#0040ff) to convey trust and professionalism, in line with established visual brands.
- Background color: Very dark gray (#121212) for a modern and sophisticated look, per the source images.
- Accent color: Electric Blue (#0040ff) for call to action buttons and highlights to suggest technological advancement.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif font; use for both headers and body, because of the frequent use of text on forms and descriptions.
- Use clean, minimalist icons relevant to branding and design.
- Form on the left and the branding Kit showcase on the right, mimicking the example's clean, divided layout
- Subtle transitions when generating the brand kit to indicate progress and add a touch of dynamism.
